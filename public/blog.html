Help Save The Environment

Our Bars Can Help Save The Environment Simply With Its Shape!

Over erosion is the number one cause of soap waste. An estimated 10%-45% of all bar soap is wasted by over erosion. Each abrasive stroke of your scrubbie, loofa, or wash cloth erodes away at your precious skin loving bar soap. This is expensive and environmentally damaging.

IllinoisJo has come up with several ingenious ways to eliminate useless and harmful erosion such as: crafting bars that present less surface area, aging our premium bars for maximum hardness, creating IllinoisJo brand rcipes that help prevent waste, and much much more.
