Perfect for hard dry feet!

Use this fantastic exfoliating recipe after soaking your feet

  • Water: 344.73 grams
  • NaOH: 118.91 grams
  • Oils: 907.18 grams (60% 76deg Coconut Oil, 40% High Oleic Sunflower Oil)
  • ES: 28.35 grams (Eucalyptus and Peppermint)
  • Additives: celery seed, himalayan sea salt, superfat at 15-18%
  • Trace: medium
  • Cure: gel for 18mins at 155-170 deg F (9mins in, 5 min rest, 6 in, rest, 3 in, rest, freeze 12 hours, shelf 6-8 weeks)
